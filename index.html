Sochi Race Week 2020 Regatta Results

Over a hundred participants gathered on the Sochi coast to compete for the main prize. Both beginners and professional sailors with considerable experience took part in the competition.

Throughout the voyage, we were accompanied by the sun and beautiful weather, on some days the wind was up to 20 knots. The regatta was well organized, 135 sailors, 7 crews, 9 participating countries, 4 racing days, 3 divisions.

Participating teams:

  • Vostok 35
  • Fortune Sailing
  • Siberia
  • Leviathan
  • Black Swan
  • White Pepper
  • Pearl

The Fortune Sailing team became the champion of the Sochi Race Week 2020 regatta, last year they stopped one step away from victory, and this season they outpaced their closest rival, the Vostok 35 crew, by 12 points 5 points.

We are very friendly in the team and understand each other without words, I think that's why we won

Congratulations to the winners and we look forward to seeing all the participants again at the autumn regattas!

Alex Newman 20.06.2021
